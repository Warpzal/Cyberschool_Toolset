West Bend School District Update Newsletter

The West Bend School District Update is published weekly (twice-monthly during the summer) and sent via email to all district families and staff. If you have a child enrolled in the school district and an email address on file with Skyward, you receive the newsletter. Review 2021-2022 issues of the West Bend School District Update.
